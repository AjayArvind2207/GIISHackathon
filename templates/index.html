Making self-isolation a fun and enjoyable experience

What Quarantine++ does best

Provide Entertainment

Quarantine++ serves as a hub for entertainment during lockdown and SHN's
It has a large collection of books, and puzzles
It also serves as a hub for weather updates and news

Emergency Contact system

In the case of any health emergency during the SHN, the individual can immediately contact the health authorities with this feature

Personalized and privatized information

All information from our app is privatized
People may sign in with their google accounts, hence increasing privacy
Furthermore, all book suggestions are individualized

The introduction of lockdowns and stay-home notices has depreciated mental health and social well-being severely.

We aim to improve this trend.

The perspective of the user

The users log-in to the website using their email IDs Once they log in, they are given the choice between the three main features our project offers

The entertainment section consists of a large number of courses, books and even movies. It also contains the news and the weather. All this information is scraped from various websites

The puzzle of the day is a unique feature which scrapes a random puzzle from the internet and resets it everyday. This can serve as an interactive medium and help to treat isolation Hence, we may assist people in these dire times in improving their mental health and leading a healthier life.
